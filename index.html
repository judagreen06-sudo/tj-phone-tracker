How TJ Phone Tracker Works

Point System

1 hour = 1 point = 1 shekel toward Wolt!

Keep your phone out of the Beis during seder times to earn points.

Seder Times & Max Points

  • Morning: 9:30 AM - 1:00 PM (3.5 pts max)
  • Afternoon: 3:00 PM - 5:45 PM (2.75 pts max)
  • Night: 8:00 PM - 10:00 PM (2.0 pts max)

No seder on Friday and Saturday (Shabbat)

How to Use

  1. Check In: Press "Check In" at the start of seder
  2. Stay Focused: Keep your phone away during learning
  3. Check Out: Press "Check Out" when done
  4. Earn Points: Points are added automatically!

Leaderboard

Compete with your chavrusas! Check the leaderboard to see:

  • Today: Points earned today
  • This Week: Points earned this week
  • All-Time: Total points earned
